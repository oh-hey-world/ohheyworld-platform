Oh Hey World

Your Personal Trusted Hospitality Network

The available accommodation options for a traveler: hotel, short term rental, hostel, private or shared room via AirBnB, or Couchsurfing with a stranger. Or you stay with friends or family.

If you think about it, almost everyone "couchsurfs" from time to time. They just happen to stay with existing trusted friends and family rather than strangers. We enable you to unlock your trusted connections to find a person to stay with anywhere in the world, directly from the palm of your hand.

Our early growth strategy is geared around reaching critical mass among several member associations with a strong shared bond that implies trust among members, even if they have not met in person. Examples of such networks are Sigma Phi Epsilon (300k+ alumni), University of Washington Bothell (12k alumni), YPO Next Gen (19k kids of YPO ages 18-35), and Impact HUB (40k paid members).
